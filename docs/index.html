Electro

MIX THE INTERNETS

with Electro download and mix from your favorite content sources. Electro uses the WebAudio API and WebRTC to giving you full control over pitch, eq and gain. Add a secondary output and cue tracks to your headphones before mixing it live.
Electro is open source! Open issues! Contribute! Tell us we suck!
